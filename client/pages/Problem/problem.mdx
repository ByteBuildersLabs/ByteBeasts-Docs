## Core Problems We're Solving

ByteBeasts addresses three fundamental interconnected problems that have limited both the mass adoption of web3 technologies and the depth of connection in digital experiences.

### 1. Emotional Disconnection with Digital Assets üíî

In traditional games, players invest significant amounts of time, effort, and resources in developing characters and collecting items, but these remain under the complete control of developers. This lack of true ownership creates a fundamentally asymmetric relationship and limits the emotional connection players can form with their digital assets.
