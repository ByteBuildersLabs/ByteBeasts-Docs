## Web3 Gaming Market Overview

The Web3 gaming market represents one of the most dynamic and rapidly growing segments within the broader gaming industry. By leveraging blockchain technology to create decentralized gaming experiences, Web3 games are fundamentally transforming how players interact with, own, and monetize their gaming experiences.
