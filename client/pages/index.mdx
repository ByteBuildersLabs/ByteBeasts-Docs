# Introduction

ByteBeasts is an innovative gaming ecosystem that combines the emotional depth of virtual pets with true ownership of digital assets on blockchain. Our first game, ByteBeasts-Tamagotchi, reimagines the classic virtual pet concept for the web3 era, allowing users to raise, care for, and evolve digital creatures (Beasts) that permanently exist on the blockchain.
